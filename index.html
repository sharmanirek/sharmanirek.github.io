My Journey

My work at Upstart makes use of my background in data science, systems engineering, and statistics, where I focus on Machine Learning Fairness. I work to detect and mitigate disparate impacts of ML models across protected groups.

Over the last handful of years, I've worked on developing and applying ML systems to solve problems that help people. But as these models have become more and more capable, I've become more passionate about addressing the unintended effects of ML models.
